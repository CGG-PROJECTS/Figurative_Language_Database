Picturebook Figurative Language Database

Research-based open resource for teachers

About This Resource

This database contains over 68,000 figurative language examples (metaphors, similes, personification, hyperbole) from thousands of picturebooks. Picturebooks have more than 5× the figurative density of child-directed speech.

Column Guide:

  • Source Chain of Thought → AI reasoning about the source domain (what concrete image is used)
  • Target Chain of Thought → AI reasoning about the target domain (what abstract idea/emotion is being described)
  • AI Short Comment → Quick quality note + simple explanation
  • Find Source → Click to search Google for the book or read-aloud video
