About This Resource

Here you can search and collect real examples of figurative language expressions for discussion, worksheets, and classroom activities. You can quickly find books and multimedia sources by clicking the link next to any metaphor, simile, personification, or hyperbole entry.

You can also select examples from the database and use AI to create activities, suggest related metaphors, explain expressions, and explore possible origin/history notes.

Try searching dream, for example. You may retrieve many expressions that show how a single concept can be figuratively elaborated in strikingly different directions: dreams appear as vast or measurable entities, as objects that can be built upon, trapped, or destroyed, and as goals that demand active pursuit.

You can also use the dropdown filters to explore metaphors by concept/domain, such as emotions, love, life and loss, health and illness, games and sport, human relationships, and communication.
