About This Resource

Here you can search and collect real examples of figurative language expressions for classroom discussion, worksheets, and learning activities. You can quickly find books and multimedia sources by clicking the link next to any metaphor, simile, personification, or hyperbole entry.

You can also select examples from the database and use AI to generate activities, suggest related metaphors, explain an expression, and explore possible origin/history notes.

Try searching dream: you may retrieve many expressions that show how one concept can be elaborated in very different figurative directions—for example, dreams as vast/measurable entities, as objects that can be built or trapped, or as goals that require action and struggle.

Use the dropdown filters to explore metaphors by concept/domain, including emotions, love, life and loss, health and illness, games and sport, human relationships, and communication.
